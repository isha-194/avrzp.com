About AVR ZP High School

AVR ZP High School is located in the village of Edlapalli and is one of the most respected educational institutions in the area. Students from more than 5 surrounding villages come to study here, making it a hub for the region's educational needs.

With over 2000 students enrolled, the school offers a spacious and well-equipped environment for learning. It has a large playground that allows students to engage in various sports activities, and a spacious parking area for bicycles.

Our highly dedicated teachers provide education in both Telugu and English mediums, ensuring that students from different linguistic backgrounds receive quality education. The school is also a center for vibrant cultural activities, with events such as dancing, sports, essay writing competitions, and much more, promoting holistic development for all students.
